JURASSIC EGG HUNTER

ATTENTION! JURASSIC PARK EGG HUNTERS

You are automatically assigned a number of precious T-Rex eggs to collect.

There are four different dinosaur eggs. By clicking on a dinosaur egg you will collect a specific amount of points to your egg amount.

You will complete your assignment by matching your egg amount to the assigned egg amount. You lose if your number goes above the assigned number. T-Rex gets VERY angery when you take too many of her precious eggs, YOU WILL BE EATEN.

The value of each egg is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when your assignment starts, the value of the egg will change.
